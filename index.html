About Inkaastra








Inkaastra is a tattoo and design studio based in Vadodara city of Gujarat State, India.

The word 'Inkaastra' is a collision of western and eastern world having Ink + Aastra (means weapon in Sanskrit), to well define the work being executed. We are a team of experienced artists offering services including Tattooing, Conceptual Design, Illustration and Cartooning. Our speciality resides in forming black and grey tattoo.
